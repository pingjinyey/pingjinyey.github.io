Zhisheng Niu received his B.E. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, China, in 1985, and his M.E. and Ph.D. degrees from Toyohashi University of Technology, Japan, in 1989 and 1992, respectively. After working at Fujitsu Laboratories Ltd., Japan (1992–1994), he joined Tsinghua University in 1994 as a Lecturer, became an Associate Professor later that year, and has been a Professor since 1999. He served as Deputy Dean for Research, School of Information Science and Technology (2004–2016), Deputy Head for Research, Department of Electronic Engineering (2003–2004), Deputy Director of the University Office of Science and Technology (2000–2002), and Chair of Graduate Students Affairs, Department of Electronic Engineering (1998–2000).

### Graduate Students Supervised

He is currently supervising a research group consisting of 5 Ph.D. and 2 Master students. Since joining Tsinghua University in 1994, he has graduated 34 Ph.D. and 27 Master students in total, among them 9 found faculty positions in the US, Europe, and China, and 4 are international students. He has also collaborated with 7 postdoctoral researchers, 5 of whom found faculty positions after completion.
