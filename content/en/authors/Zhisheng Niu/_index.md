Zhisheng Niu graduated from Beijing Jiaotong University, China, in 1985, and received his M.E. and Ph.D. degrees from Toyohashi University of Technology, Japan, in 1989 and 1992, respectively. He joined Tsinghua University in 1994 and has been a Professor since 1999.

He is an IEEE Fellow (Class 2012) and IEICE Fellow (Class 2007). He served as Editor-in-Chief of IEEE Trans. on Green Communications and Networking (2020-2022) and is currently a Senior Editor for IEEE JSAC. He received the Harold Sobol Award from IEEE ComSoc in 2019.

His research interests include green communications, queueing theory, traffic engineering, and network resource optimization.
