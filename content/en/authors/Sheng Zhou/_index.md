Dr. Sheng Zhou received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2005 and 2011, respectively. During his doctoral studies, he was a visiting student at the Wireless System Lab, Department of Electrical Engineering, Stanford University (Jan.–Jun. 2010), hosted by Prof. Andrea Goldsmith. From November 2013 to January 2014, he was a Visiting Researcher at the Central Research Lab of Hitachi Ltd., Japan. He joined the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in 2011, first as a postdoctoral scholar, then as an Assistant Professor in 2013, and became a tenured Associate Professor in 2019.
