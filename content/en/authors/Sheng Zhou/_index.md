Sheng Zhou received his B.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees from Tsinghua University in 2005 and 2011, respectively. He was a visiting student at Stanford University (Jan–Jun 2010, advisor: Prof. Andrea Goldsmith). He is currently a Tenured Associate Professor in the Dept. of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University.

He received the IEEE ComSoc Asia-Pacific Outstanding Paper Award (2024) and the ComSoc Wireless TC Outstanding Young Researcher Award (2020). He serves as Editor for IEEE Trans. Wireless Commun. (2021–present) and is Head of the Institute of Communications, Dept. EE, Tsinghua University (since Aug 2023).

His research focuses on mobile edge intelligence, vehicular networks, green wireless communications, and low-altitude intelligent networks.
