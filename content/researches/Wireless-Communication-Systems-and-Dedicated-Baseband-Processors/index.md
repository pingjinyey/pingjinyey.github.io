Our work spans LTE-V/C-V2X vehicular communication, 230 MHz power private network baseband chips, and baseband ASICs for roadside units and industrial IoT. Building on RISC-V and dataflow-driven architectures, we develop domain-specific processors for key communication kernels such as synchronization, channel estimation, and MIMO detection, together with supporting software and toolchains.
